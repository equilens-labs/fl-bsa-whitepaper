
\section{Statistical Analysis Plan (Summary)}
\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*]
\item Significance level $\alpha=0.05$.
\item Selection rates: Wilson confidence intervals.
\item AIR (\cref{eq:air}): delta-method confidence interval on $\log(\mathrm{AIR})$ (deterministic).
\item SRG (\cref{eq:srg}): Newcombe/Wilson confidence interval for a difference of proportions.
\item P-values: two-proportion z-test by default; Fisher exact under the small-n policy.
\item Multiplicity (race pairs): Holm--Bonferroni adjustment on p-values (CIs unadjusted).
\end{itemize}
