
\section{Results}
\subsection{Fairness}
\noindent \Cref{tab:gender_air_slices} is the main scenario readout. The historical fixture reports AIR \GenderAIRHistorical{} for \GenderProtectedGroup{} vs \GenderReferenceGroup{}, below the \AirMin{} four-fifths threshold. The amplification (bias-preserving) slice reproduces the same level of disparity at AIR \GenderAIRAmplification{}, showing that the branch preserves rather than launders the bias signal. The intrinsic (de-biased) slice restores the ratio to AIR \GenderAIRIntrinsic{}, which localizes the disparity to the decision labels in this fixture. The result should be read as a positive-control pass for detection and branch separation, not as a customer portfolio finding.
