\begin{abstract}
We present a regulator-ready, statistically rigorous evaluation of Equilens' Fair Lending Bias-Simulation Appliance (FL-BSA). The paper formalises core fairness and calibration estimands---including the Adverse Impact Ratio (AIR), Equalised Odds (EO), and the Expected Calibration Error (ECE)---and specifies a Statistical Analysis Plan (SAP) based on Wilson score intervals for proportions and bias-corrected and accelerated (BCa) bootstrap confidence intervals for derived metrics. We describe the data-privacy and provenance boundaries, and demonstrate the framework on a synthetic fair-lending audit scenario, reporting quantified uncertainty for key metrics in a form suitable for regulatory review.
\end{abstract}
