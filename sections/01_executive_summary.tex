
\section{Executive Summary}

\subsection{Scope and Positioning}
This whitepaper documents the \ScenarioLabel{} fixture and the evidence artefacts generated by FL-BSA, a self-hosted fair-outcomes evidence appliance for regulated credit decisions. It is intended for technical, governance, and model-risk reviewers at regulated credit providers, credit-infrastructure firms, and implementation partners. It provides metrics and documentation building blocks aligned with the Equal Credit Opportunity Act (\ECOA) and Regulation B (\RegB) \citep{ecoa_regb}, the \EUAIAct{} Article 10 data-governance requirements \citep{eu_ai_act}, and related frameworks. It is not a legal opinion and does not constitute a complete compliance submission for any specific deployment.

\noindent The FL-BSA v5.0.0 release referenced by this paper is a technical/native appliance stable release. The release evidence for this version remains \texttt{customer\_evidence\_eligible=false} and \texttt{customer\_evidence\_disposition=characterization\_only}. This paper does not approve customer evidence, vendor-authored evidence, legal/compliance certification, broad commercial authorization, public Marketplace go-live, production live decisioning, near-duplicate privacy, formal differential privacy, complete protected-class/intersection coverage, or regulator approval. Customer-evidence approval requires a later exact-SHA signed release-owner record verified against the pinned trust root and an approved delivery route.

\subsection{Purpose}
This paper is intended to help technical and governance reviewers interpret the synthetic audit evidence generated for \ScenarioLabel{}. We evaluate selection-rate fairness and quantify uncertainty, surfacing violations when present. We report a disparity ratio (AIR-equivalent) and approval-rate gaps against pre-specified thresholds.\footnote{Thresholds are specified in the SAP for this scenario; see Appendix~A.}
