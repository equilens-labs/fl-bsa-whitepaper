
\section{Security and Privacy}
In the configured self-hosted/no-egress posture, raw customer data remains inside client-controlled boundaries by default. Optional marketplace metering, when enabled, sends aggregate usage counts only. Provenance captures software container digests and dataset hashes per run. This intake does not claim differential privacy accounting; the manifest records \texttt{not\_differential\_privacy}, and the synthetic-quality certificates state that near-duplicate privacy and differential privacy are not claimed.
